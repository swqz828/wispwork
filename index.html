about me

Hi! I'm Selina Zhai, a first-year computer engineering student at the University of Waterloo with a love for the intersection of art and technology. This portfolio is a collection of things I've explored — from stop-motion videos to UI/UX projects — and I'm always looking to add more to it.

Over the upcoming months, I want to dive deeper into hardware because there's something so satisfying about making things with your hands and ending up with a tactile, physical result. I've dabbled in Arduino projects before and had a lot of fun, so I'm excited to see where this goes!

In my free time you'll find me reading manga, crocheting, or getting a little too invested in legal dramas. I love trying new things — which honestly is what led me to most of the projects on this page — so be on the lookout for future random projects! Glad you're here, and I hope you enjoy looking around!
